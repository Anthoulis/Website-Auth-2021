Who we are

We are four Computer Science students who were given the opportunity to raise our voice about a matter that strongly saddens us. We took this opportunity to make a WebSite that will empower our voice about the injustice many women experience daily in their lives. We deeply hope to make a stand and support , empower , listen to women all around the world.

Additionally, through our WebPage we opt to educate young people - boys & girls - on how to eliminate patterns of gender inequality.

Meet us below. Feel free to contact each and every one of us on any matter that concerns you.
